About Me

Hello! I'm Aryan Basel, a Mechanical Engineering student at Kathmandu University with a focus on sustainable design and Automobile. My journey in engineering began with a childhood fascination with taking things apart to understand how they work.

Today, I combine analytical thinking with creative problem-solving to design mechanical systems that are efficient, sustainable, and innovative. I'm particularly interested in renewable energy technologies and smart manufacturing processes.

When I'm not in the lab or working on projects, you can find me participating in robotics competitions, volunteering for STEM outreach programs, or exploring hiking trails with my camera. I am also a creative hunk, having worked in organizing world class events such as TedX events.
